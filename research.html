Research

Projects & Publications

My research sits at the intersection of political sociology and social movement studies, with a focus on protest policing, authoritarian governance, and collective action. Below are my current and completed projects.

Working Paper · Under Review

Protest Policing Research: Cases, Explanations and the Language of a Field

Co-authored with Dr. Howard Ramos. A systematic scoping review of the protest policing literature, mapping cases, theoretical explanations, and the conceptual language shaping the field.

Working Paper · 2026

Discursive Opportunities and Demands for Protest Regulation

Evidence from Toronto's Public Order Policing Consultations, examining how discursive political opportunities shape demands for protest regulation in a Canadian urban context.

Publication · 2024

February 6 Earthquake Report: Institutions, Irregularities and Transparency

Published with Transparency International (TI). Examines institutional responses, irregularities, and transparency issues following the 2023 earthquake in Turkey.

Read report →

Publication · 2023

Global Healthcare Industry and Medical Tourism Trends

Co-authored industry report published with Grant Thornton, examining global trends in healthcare and medical tourism with policy and market implications.

Conference Presentation · 2025

Unpacking Policing and Protest: A Scoping Review

Poster presented at the Western University Sociology Graduate Students Association (SGSA) Annual Conference, London, Ontario, April 2025.

M.A. Thesis · 2021

Politicization, Radicalization and Moderation: Repertoires of Action in Islamist Youth

Comparative study of Turkish and Tunisian Islamist youth movements, examining how political contexts shape the construction of repertoires of collective action.
