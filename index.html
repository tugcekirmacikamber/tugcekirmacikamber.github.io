Tuğce Kırmacı Kamber

Ph.D. Candidate · Department of Sociology · Western University, Ontario

I study the evolving relationship between protest and state power in contemporary democracies. My research explores how shifts toward far right and authoritarian governance reshape the policing of dissent and influence protest behaviour. I work with quantitative, qualitative, and computational methods, drawing on political sociology and social movement studies, and I am broadly interested in how states manage and respond to collective action.
